Meaghan Doherty

A multidisciplinary strategist working on civic technology, open source communities, and storytelling with data.

Currently, a product design strategist with WONDROS, Meag leads strategy for open data research tools at All of Us Research Program.
Previously, Meag has led design research and strategy for clients including, OpenGov Foundation, National Park Foundation, Centers for Medicare and Medicaid, Rock the Vote, and Small Business Innovation Research.
Outside of her design research practice, Meag mentors open source developers and organizers at Mozilla Foundation, a Co-Founder Tech, Rebalanced, a community day for women, nonbinary, and trans individuals in the DC tech community, and a Captain for Code for DC.
Meag started her career at Partners Healthcare's Center for Connected Health exploring efficacy of emerging digital health tools and devices like Proteus, Reflexion Health, and FitBit.
