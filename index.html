Meag is a Washington, D.C. based designer, web developer, and social justice advocate. Her work crosses multiple disciplines, with experience with the OpenGov Foundation, U.S. Congress, Salesforce, and most recently, New America Foundation's Open Technology Institute.

With an undergraduate degree in public health and 300 training hours in web development, Meag believes the only way to get things done is together.
